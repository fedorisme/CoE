George Zacharopoulos

Greece

George is an international stand up comedian from Greece who has lived in the UK for over 15 years.

He is a regular headliner in the UK comedy scene and had performed in festivals across the world, such as

  • Melbourne International Comedy Festival
  • Perth Fringe World
  • Adelaide Festival
  • Athens Comedy Festival
  • Edinburgh festival

George has also appeared on TV, for BBC and Sky.
