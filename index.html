The Best European Stand Up in English

Comedians of Europe is a one-of-a-kind collaboration between some of the finest English-speaking European comedians. A unique brand that celebrates out Pan-European diversity through laughter.

Not only do we organize live comedy shows in English throughout Europe and at many major international comedy festivals, but we also offer comedians for corporate events. And don’t forget our online shows.

Feel free to contact us about shows, bookings or anything else.

Comedians of Europe is an amazing resource for corporate comedy.

It takes a very experienced comedian to entertain a diverse group of people, create an environment that makes a corporate audience laugh freely AND set the organizer at ease, safe in the knowledge that the material will be targeted, smart and appropriate for the group.

Unified by our skill set, but diversified by our offering, Comedians of Europe offers a unique opportunity to create a show with anything from one to four of our comedians.

The diversity of our group creates a unique opportunity to truly tailor the night to your brand or event. And ensure that your corporate event is a massive hit.

Listening is the cost of being heard:
We will work with you to choose the comedians that are perfect for your event to make sure that your event is special, memorable and bonding.

There is nothing that unites us like laughter. And while laughing alone is great, very few things feel as special as laughing together.

Call us and let’s chat about your event.
