Steve Hili

Malta

Steve is a fun, high-energy, award-winning comedian and MC from the tiny island Malta. Moving to the UK a few years ago, Steve’s style merges his sunny island roots with his cheeky London wit.

Equally confident improvising with his audience or sharing an outrageous story, Steve has the energy to lift a room and leave it gasping for more. He performs regularly at the Edinburgh fringe and in comedy clubs across the UK, Europe, and in Australia.

Steve has appeared on and/or written for, BBC, Sky, Discovery, and SBS (Melbourne). He currently presents a daily show on XFM.
