Kristýna Haklová

Czech Republic

Kristýna comes from the small town of Beroun in Central Bohemia. From there she went to Masaryk University in Brno where she fought for several years to get her useless degree in Film Studies, only to end up as a telemarketer. Sound familiar? As you're correctly assuming right now, yes, she wanted to have a gap year to travel around Australia! But Australia decided it didn't need her there. Did she give up? NO! She ended up in Auckland, New Zealand, overstayed her visa for two years, at the end of which she nearly died of boredom. She is currently working as an art house film distributor and stand-up comedy promoter/performer.

She is back in Europe, performing mainly in Berlin and Prague, never mentioning the above stories, only complaining about single life, her drinking habits and the generally low quality of porn.
